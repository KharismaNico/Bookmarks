Welcome to the lbry.org Wiki!

This Wiki is the knowledge base for the LBRY Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to the growth, development, and adoption of LBRY in a bottom-up, community-driven fashion. The Foundation also exists to ensure that LBRY governance and growth is as decentralized as the protocol itself.

The Foundation was formed at the end of 2019 as the next evolution of the LBRY Swarm, a collaboration of LBRY users, creators, developers, fans, and more from around the world.

Want to meet us? Come hang out in our chat.
