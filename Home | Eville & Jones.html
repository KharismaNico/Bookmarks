Welcome to Eville & Jones

Eville & Jones is the leading provider of out-sourced official veterinary controls throughout Europe. Our staff deliver audit, verification and enforcement services in the fields of animal health, public health and animal welfare.

We can tailor a staffing solution that will meet all your needs, whether short or long term, and in any European country delivered on a timescale that meets your needs.

Having worked in a number of member states we are expert at creating innovative solutions that meet all legislative requirements on a rapid and cost effective basis. With a team of experts in charge of delivery your satisfaction can be guaranteed.

If there are any questions about our services don’t hesitate to contact us.

Meet the Team

The company has a wide range of skills and expertise contained within its personal base: employing over 500 staff ensures that most skill requirements can be met directly from within the team. The company has the industry’s highest staff retention statistics and this is testament to the core loyalty and commitment that the company engenders. Many of the key staff are recognised as being industry leaders and are well connected to key decision-makers throughout Europe and further afield. The strength of the company lies in the core capability and competence of its staff and the management team.
