This is the personal Web site of Iris Morelle, also known in some corners of the Web as shadowm or ShikadiQueen. Her original intention was to use this space to include a professional-sounding introductory blurb for casual visitors, but half-way through she realized that writing about herself in third person on her own Web site just does not work.

Here you may find information about my open-source software endeavors, my opinions and anecdotes, and, of course, me. I even left instructions for contacting me if you ever feel so inclined!
