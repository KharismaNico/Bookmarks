Why we do it

Reclaiming our ability to listen to the teachings whispered by our blood, as the key to healthy bodies, restoring nature and good community.

Reason one

In a rapidly changing future, skills like self-awareness, emotional intelligence and having right relationship will determine whether we will survive or thrive.

Reason two

By re-connecting to nature, remembering we are a part of her – not separate, by becoming comfortable in her embrace – we remember more of who we are and are empowered in our everyday lives.

Reason three

Humans are wired for connection – to each other and the world around us; this is how we grow, learn and heal. Contemporary ‘civilisation’ is not necessary designed to support this truth.
