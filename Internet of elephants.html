groundbreaking tools for consumer engagement with wildlife


In the past 40 years, the world has lost 50% of its wildlife. Without effectively attracting and activating the next generation of potential difference makers to the wildlife conservation landscape, the next 100 years of wildlife conservation will look much the same as it does now - an uphill losing battle, supported only by the will of the very few. We are a team of wildlife lovers, data heads and gaming geeks who use the unique combination of their skills to support conservation.

our work

 

Imagine a new way of engaging the public with wildlife conservation - one in which real wildlife supported by real conservation organizations, became part of their daily life! We use the latest technology to build innovative and engaging digital experiences that create closer relationships between people and wildlife, whether it is through Augmented Reality, location based games or data visualisations. These are some of our first products:

 

Satellite Stories

Get a birds eye view of a week in the life of wildlife living at Ol Pejeta Conservancy.

Safari Central

Spot an elephant walking down your street, take a selfie with a pangolin: Safari Central brings wildlife into your life.

Stories of the Wild

Eavesdrop on the lives of three animals living in the wild, as told through their data.
