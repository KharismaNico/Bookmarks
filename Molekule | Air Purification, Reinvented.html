PECO: the clear winner against HEPA.

Mold destruction

Mold spreads through the air and finds surfaces to grow on. While HEPA technology can catch mold, its filters also can become places for mold growth. Eventually, this mold can escape back into the air. Because of Molekule’s breakthrough PECO purification process, mold is destroyed in the air as it passes through the device.

VOCs destruction

PECO destroys volatile organic compounds (VOCs), which are too small to be captured by even the best-in-class HEPA technology. Carbon filtration technology, while able to capture some chemicals initially, can re-emit these same chemicals back into the air.

Allergens destruction

PECO breaks down even the smallest allergens, like pollen, dust mites, and dander to provide truly clean air.

Virus destruction

Viruses are so small they can easily escape HEPA-grade filters, but even if caught on the filter, they can remain viable for up to 200 days. PECO offers a revolutionary and effective method for destroying virus pollutants. Molekule can eliminate millions of airborne viruses that are brought indoors.

Bacteria destruction

Bacteria can easily stay alive on traditional HEPA filters. Studies have shown that bacteria can multiply on these filters and can escape back into the air. Even if the bacteria die, they can still release endotoxins into the air if not properly eliminated. Molekule can destroy even the smallest bacteria before they have a chance to accumulate.
