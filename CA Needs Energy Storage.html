CA Needs Energy Storage
Let’s Get More Reliable, Renewable Power on the Grid

California has ambitious clean energy goals: 100 percent carbon-free electricity by 2045.

Wind and solar power provide a lot of clean energy, but they’re not always available. The Golden State needs a clean energy solution that can provide clean, renewable power at all times of the day or night. Energy storage is the solution. It provides a way to get more affordable green energy on the grid whenever it’s needed.

LET YOUR ELECTED OFFICIALS KNOW YOU SUPPORT ENERGY STORAGE SOLUTIONS THAT WILL CREATE A CLEANER, MORE RELIABLE POWER GRID FOR ALL CALIFORNIANS.

HELP CALIFORNIA SECURE A RELIABLE AND CLEAN ENERGY FUTURE

Renewable energy resources create major challenges to providing reliable electricity to California homes and businesses. We need new solutions.

Energy storage technologies – both short-duration solutions like batteries as well as long-duration solutions such as pumped hydro and compressed air – will help ensure Californians have access to clean energy around-the-clock.

New clean energy projects will create good-paying jobs and economic opportunity for local businesses in areas that need them the most.

The construction and operation of major energy infrastructure projects, such as bulk energy storage, create thousands of jobs and support local economies. The renewable energy sector has already created more than a half million clean energy jobs across California in all 58 counties.
