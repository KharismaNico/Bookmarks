PC/104 Products

To meet the unique requirements of the ever-expanding embedded application marketplace, Connect Tech offers a range of cost-effective, high performance multi-port serial cards that are fully PC/104 compliant.

Connect Tech offers a robust line of PC/104 products including PCI/104 Express, PCIe/104, PC/104-Plus, PCI/104 and PC/104. Explore the serial boards, adapters, carrier boards, and single board computers available through Connect Tech. Compact in form but sacrificing nothing in power and reliability, our PC/104 products offer flexible, quality options for your embedded communications needs.
