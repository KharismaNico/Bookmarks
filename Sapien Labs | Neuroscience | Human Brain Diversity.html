NEW

  • Introducing the Mental Health Quotient (MHQ)

The human brain is an experience dependent organ, its dynamics as varied as our life experiences. We seek to enhance human well being through a deep and inclusive understanding of the diversity of the human brain and its function. We build tools for the research community to accelerate and amplify progress, and enable new research programs in underrepresented regions of the world.

THE HUMAN BRAIN
DIVERSITY PROJECT

The human brain diversity project is a large scale global effort to build a database of EEG data along with extensive standardized metadata relating to environment and lifestyle and cognitive and mental health across the lifespan and across the globe.

Learn More

NEUROLAB PROGRAM

The Neurolab program equips researchers in Asia, Africa and Latin America working with underrepresented human populations in adjacent fields such as public health, nutrition and education with the tools, equipment and tutorials to add EEG and brain assessments to their field research.

Learn More

RESEARCH TOOLS

We build research tools that enable large scale field assessment of brain health and function to power the Human Brain Diversity Project and beyond. These include cognitive and mental health assessment tools, novel EEG analytical pipelines and the spin out research collaboration and data management platform Brainbase.
